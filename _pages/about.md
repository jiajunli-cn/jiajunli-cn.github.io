## Featured Research

<div class="research-card">
  <p class="research-card__meta">Working paper</p>
  <h3>The Long Shadow of Heroes: Martyrs' Cemetery, Patriotic Education, and Street Crime</h3>
  <p>This project examines how political memory embedded in public space shapes social order. Using geo-coded crime data and a staggered Difference-in-Differences design, I show that Martyrs' Cemetery Parks are associated with lower nearby crime, with effects that vary across space and commemorative periods.</p>
  <p><a href="https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5866022">Read paper</a></p>
</div>

<div class="research-card">
  <p class="research-card__meta">Working paper</p>
  <h3>Reticence or Theatrics? Grassroots Cadre Presence and Political Response in Surveys</h3>
  <p>This paper studies how the presence of grassroots cadres changes responses to political survey questions. Drawing on the China Family Panel Studies and simulation-based evidence, I argue that cadre presence generates social desirability bias through authoritarian cognition rather than simple silence or item nonresponse.</p>
  <p><a href="https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5295959">Read paper</a></p>
</div>
