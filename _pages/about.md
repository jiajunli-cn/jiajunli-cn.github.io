## Featured Research

<div class="research-card">
  <p class="research-card__meta">Working paper</p>
  <h3>The Long Shadow of Heroes: Martyrs' Cemetery, Patriotic Education, and Street Crime</h3>
  <p>This project examines how political memory embedded in public space shapes social order. Using geocoded crime data and a staggered Difference-in-Differences design, I find that Martyrs' Cemetery Parks reduce nearby crime, especially property crime, with effects varying across distance and commemorative periods.</p>
  <p><a href="https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5866022">Read paper</a></p>
</div>

<div class="research-card">
  <p class="research-card__meta">Working paper</p>
  <h3>Reticence or Theatrics? Grassroots Cadre Presence and Political Response in Surveys</h3>
  <p>This paper studies how grassroots cadre presence changes responses to sensitive survey questions. Using four waves of CFPS (2012-2018), RAG and in-context-learning simulations, and implicit association tests, I show that responses shift toward politically preferred answers through authoritarian cognition.</p>
  <p><a href="https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5295959">Read paper</a></p>
</div>
